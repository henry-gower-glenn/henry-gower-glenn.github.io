Emotional Intelligence

What is emotional intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is when you have a good understanding of your emotions and can use them effectively to tackle challenges.

How is it different to IQ?

It is different because IQ is using logic and problem-solving skills to tackle problems whereas emotional intelligence is focusing more on how your emotions effect your decision making and learning.

Why is it important?

Emotional intelligence is important because it provides people with better decision making. Using your emotions to your advantage can get you much further than if you choose to ignore them.
