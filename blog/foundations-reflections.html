Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practising programming instead? Justify your answer.



I understand that Core Learning is helpful and will be essential when looking for jobs. But the time frames need to be laid out better. I think Core leaning has too many hours invested into it. As a student that has come into this course with zero skills in coding, I would very much rather use the extra time to learn the technical parts. I have also done other courses in life that try to make people self aware and talk about their values etc but at the end of the day I would very much rather just be learning what I wanted to sign up to this course for, and that is CODING. To sum up: I do believe what was taught in the Core Values part of foundations can be neccasary but it took up to much time in my opinion and I would prefer to invest atleast half of the hours invested in Core learning to be invested in Tech learning.
